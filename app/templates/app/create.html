Content creation on Pigeon is a multi-step process.

  1. First, members of the community add headlines to the Headline Queue.
  2. Next, anyone can select a headline and begin an article draft. Multiple contributors can work on the same article draft at the same time, in a real-time collaborative editor.
  3. Finally, the draft is submitted for review by a professional editor.
